Caroline, 21

Hello world, and welcome to my first website! This is a 90's inspired project I am working on, while studying interaction design in Oslo. On this site you can read about how to live your best life, the right way. Also called The Caroline Way. We will go through a brief history, some fun-facts, and then the ten commandments. Good luck, and enjoy! All the statements you are about to read are in no way or form representet by Kristiania, sorry for the inconvenience.

"Man maa ingenting"

I was born on april fool's day in 1998, way up north. My family and myself are originally sapmi people, but I don't speak a word sapmi. I do however speak spanish fluently, because I was studying in Madrid before moving to Oslo. I have been playing the french horn for 10 years, and I started shooting guns when I was twelve. I started studying IxD because I am a creative person that finds technology interesting. I am hoping to become a kick-ass interaction designer that can code fast and make everything look aesthetically pleasing. So far it is not looking promising.

The ten commandments

  • Don't bleach your hair, it will also break you.
